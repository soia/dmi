What is DMIcoin?

DMI is a cryptocurrency collaboration between DMI and Melaka Straits City in providing an open source peer-to-peer digital currency. The DMI platform aims to empower individuals and institutions from around the world to invest in the DMIcoin.

The coins will be used in Melaka Straits City for specific functions. This gives the coins some functionality. It will mainly be used in payment of government-based services in the city.

DMI will also feature an exchange that can allow tourists who visit Melaka Straits City to exchange their fiat currencies for DMI coins that they can use freely.
